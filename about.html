What is FASHIONVALET?

One of the largest fashion sites in South East Asia (SEA). FashionValet is the pioneer multi-label online site stocking over 400 fashion designers and brands from SEA. Established in November 2010, FV curates the best selection of fashion and beauty brands under one roof, bridging them to customers all over the world.The first flagship retail store is at Bangsar Village,KL. Started as an e-commerce only brand, The FashionValet Group today has opened 10 retail stores and counting, with the hopes to become the leading omni channel platform by its constant technology innovation and enhancements.

FashionValet founded by popular blogger Vivy Yusof and her husband Dato Fadzarudin Shah Anuar in 2010 when they were 23 years old. Following the success of FashionValet, Vivy Yusof launched dUCk on May 5th 2014 to convey the message that wearing scarves should be a celebrated ct among woman whether Muslim or not and since then has risen quickly in its popularity among scarf-wearers. Unique in presentation and packaging, dUCk is known to be different from the rest, something the team at dUCk take very seriously.

How can come up with the idea of FASHIONVALET?

Vivy Yusof and her husband studied at UK where online consumerism was a part of daily life. They enjoyed the convenience of shopping online and it soon took over the way they conventionally shopped. After graduating university and returning to Malaysia, they realised that e-commerce was non-existent at home back in 2010. After being frustrated at the lack of online shopping option available to Malaysians and always stuck in traffic jam and crowded parking lot, they decided to find out the solution. They talked to local designers and realised they were having challenges in reaching out to the masses and developing a solid customer base. Then, they create FashionValet to push Malaysia towards expanding its consumer habits, but also to promote local designers and their immense talent.
